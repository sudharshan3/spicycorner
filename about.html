Food

Spicy Corner is your go-to online outlet for delicious veg burgers, pizzas, and sandwiches, offering mouthwatering flavors delivered straight to your door. Indulge in a variety of vegetarian treats crafted with the freshest ingredients, all from the comfort of your home! This location is situated on Kelamangalam 'X' Road, right off the bustling Hosur Road. It's easy to spot as it's located near the well-known Bajaj showroom in Kelamangalam. The area is well-connected and serves as a convenient landmark for those looking to visit. Whether you're in the mood for a quick bite or a full meal, Spicy Corner's vibrant menu of veg burgers, pizzas, and sandwiches awaits just a short distance away from this popular spot.
